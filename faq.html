Frequently Asked Questions

How does $GRUMPY work?

1. Buy on Uniswap, Sushiswap, or Dharma.
2. Hold $GRUMPY and get rewarded! 1% of every transaction is distributed to all existing $GRUMPY shareholders instantly and automatically with no fees. Sit back and watch your holdings grow!
3. As people will buy and sell $GRUMPY, with each transaction, a 1% fee is charged in $GRUMPY. The $GRUMPY fee is re-distributed to all existing $GRUMPY hodlers instantly and automatically, directly to their wallets! There are no fees for the distribution, just HODL; sit back and watch your holdings grow.

When will grumpy be listed on another exchange?

We are working very hard with several exchanges to provide more ways to buy grumpy. This process can take time, and we appreciate your patience. The best way to stay up to date on all news $GRUMPY is to follow our Twitter and join our Telegram.

Do you have any Marketing push/department?

Yes! Our marketing team is composed of seasoned professionals from different fields, from design to journalism and beyond! We are all doing volunteer work for charity. We are in talks with exchanges, shelters and charity-minded influencers to bring visibility to our project. If you have any ideas to share with us contact us directly on Telegram and let us know!

Who Are you?

We are Grumpy.finance, a decentralized and global community helping animals who need it most. Our mission is to bring the world a new use case for Cryptocurrencies, and we're just getting started.

What is Grumpy Finance, exactly?

$GRUMPY is a fair distribution token. No IDO, Private Sale, NOTHING. The best and only way tokens should be released so everyone has the same access.

How does $GRUMPY give back?

$GRUMPY has a plan! The plan has three main pillars:

1. Transparency – the community members will always know what $GRUMPY has in the works. The Community itself will have a voice in what $GRUMPY does next. This is a Community-owned decentralized project.

2. Community – “All for one and one for all”. Our community will always decide on what charities $GRUMPY donates to, comment on roadmaps and have a genuine impact on the project’s future.

3. Donations – We collect donations sent to an ERC20 wallet. The Community can track the wallet on etherscan.io Contributions to the wallet will grow and receive the same 1% passive income until $GRUMPY distributes the funds to the charity of choice. $GRUMPY aims to donate to an animal shelter or helping facility at least once every month.

How do I know when a donation has been made?

Members can track the ERC20 wallet accumulating donations and passive income on etherscan.io. It’s always lovely to follow an account knowing you donated, so please donate and track. Once the donation period is closed, $GRUMPY will post about it ahead of time. The team will complete the necessary donation forms for the selected charity, send the donation and post proof for everyone to see. Where possible, we’ll work with the charity to confirm that they have received your donations on their social media.

What are the Tokenomics?

The Original supply is 100 Trillion and part of it is locked in liquidity. There will be no minting and no burning. 1% of Each Transaction is re-distributed automatically to all existing $GRUMPY holders with no fees.

When was the $Grumpy token born?

Grumpy was created in early February 2021, but sadly the project was abandoned by its developer soon after that for personal reasons. On the 13th of March 2021 however, a community effort was made to adopt and revitalize the project. We started from scratch finding help from volunteers coming by, it's been a wild ride.

How can my shelter become eligible for a donation?

Any animal shelter with an Etherium wallet can become eligible for donation. Email us more information about your shelter so that we can share it with the community when voting occurs. (Need help setting up a wallet?)
